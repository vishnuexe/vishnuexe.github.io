About

Research-driven, product-minded

I'm a PhD scholar in Artificial Intelligence at the Indian Institute of Technology, Palakkad, where my research centers on deepfake and face-forgery detection, explainable AI, and generalizable computer-vision models.

Alongside research, I'm the Co-founder & CTO of Deepflow, where we build the next generation of explainable AI tools for real-world applications. I enjoy bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and dependable engineering.

I've also been an active volunteer and mentor — with the Research Affairs Council at IIT Palakkad, IEEE, and the National Service Scheme — and I care about making AI both useful and understandable.
